Common questions

A place isn't in the search.
In Submit, tap "Can't find it? Add it by address" and type the venue's name and street address (it's usually printed on the matchbook). We'll place it on the map for everyone.

How do rankings work?
Your head-to-head picks decide the order — rank a spot and it lands exactly where you placed it. Spots you collected from the map but haven't compared yet wait in the Unranked section until you rank them.

Who can see my collection?
Only people you approve by them following you — followers see your ranked list; everyone else sees just your name, photo, and city. City and World boards show anonymous averages.

How do I delete my account?
In the app: Profile → Delete account. It permanently erases your profile, collection, rankings, follows, and photos. Details in our privacy policy.
